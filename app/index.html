About Us Because Life Matters
 
Eastview Baptist Church is a Southern Baptist Church located on the north end of Springfield, IL. We have a small but active church with an active youth/adult ministry, children's programs, weekly craft ladies events, a fun Red Hat group, scrapbooking nights, counseling services, and much more.e
